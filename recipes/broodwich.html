The Broodwich

The best damn sandwich you'll ever eat, as seen in Vogue.

Lacks bacon because no swine is evil enough to be sacrificed upon a bed of evil....and lettuce.

Ingredients

  • Bread forged in darkness from wheat harvested from Hell's Half-Acre and baked by Beezlebub
  • Mayonnaise made from the evil eggs of a powerful dark chicken beaten into sauce by the hands of a one-eyed madman
  • Cheese boiled from the rancid teat of a fanged cow
  • 666 separate meats from an animal which has maggots for blood
  • Dijon Mustard
  • Lettuce
  • Sun-dried Tomatoes
  • Bacon is extra
